Wat is ShopChap?

Finance is everything! Money makes the world spin, and so managing it becomes ever so important. That's where SmartFinance comes in! Keeping track of your expenses has never been easier. Simply enter your income and finance, and the application will display usefull metrics and keep track of your personal finances.

Waarom deze applicatie?

Managing your wealth can be quite a daunting endevour; it may not always be that easy to answer the question: "how much do I actually spend on X?" That is a question we have all asked ourselves at some point. While most of what we buy can be traced back in our bank account, there we only see the numbers. But these numbers can be shown in a much more meaningfull way...

That is why SmartFinance was developed. Anyone who cares about keeping track of their spending can benefit from this application, as it features advanced analytical metrics that display your income and spendinghabits in a meaningfull way. Just input your expenses and the application will perform all the calculations for you, combined with usefull visualisations for a clean overview.
